Our Story

KodeQ is headquartered out of Atlanta, GA. Our development center is nestled in the heart of Ho Chi Minh city, the emerging Silicon Valley of South East Asia.

Having noticed multiple issues with trust, quality, and communication in offshore development, in 2014, our founders set out to rethink the the model itself. We wanted to put control into the hands of our clients and deliver the best talents to them. We also wanted to connect world-class developers to world-class work environment where they exchange knowledge internationally and excel. That's why KodeQ was born.

Beginning in 2015, KodeQ has developed into a house of dynamic forces that combine talent, experience, and passion. We're continuing to innovate and create new ways of doing things that aim to disrupt this decades-old industry.
