Services

We have our developers working and communicating directly with our clients’ engineering team. We use Agile processes and professional tools such as Jira and Slack to keep track of development progress and better collaborate with our clients.

  • Consulting

    Understanding your needs

    First of all, we learn about your business and your project in order to understand your development needs and to determine if KodeQ will be a good fit. If we think we can work together, we’ll work with you to develop a proposal.

    The proposal serves as a broad overview of your software project’s features and a corresponding time and budget estimate

  • Product planning

    Creating the right solution

    Before development begins, we help you plan your software product. We want to make sure we build the most valuable product features first so you don’t waste any money on unnecessary features.

  • Project management

    A collaborative workflow

    At KodeQ, we focus on the larger process of conceptualizing ideas, building workflows, discussing risks, and sketching out solutions. We’re ready to collaborate with you and your in-house staff to solidify a development process that works best for you.

    Minimizing re-work and increasing understanding

    Sometimes requirements can be misinterpreted. We try to minimize this by using the right collaboration process and seeking regular feedback. Our weekly retrospective meetings are to make sure that there will never be any surprises, just working software.

  • Development

    Building your product

    We build your product incrementally, one fully-tested and well-designed feature at a time. We add new value to the project at each iteration. Our test-driven development ensures that software is always fully tested.

    Predictable delivery

    We use project management tools that allow you to manage the backlog of features alongside with your development team, so you can modify your development priorities any time to meet your business goals. We also inform you about the progress of your software project and the status of your budget.

    Our developers will work with you directly to align expectations and leave little room for surprises, producing overall better quality software and a more transparent working relationship.

  • Delivery & support

    In it for the long haul

    Whether you’re a startup or have your own technical workforce, we’re available for the long-term to maintain and extend your application. We always shoot for very low maintenance cost, the quality of our work means you can count on us as a long-term partner.
